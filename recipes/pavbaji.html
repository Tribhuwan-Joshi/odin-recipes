Pavbaji

Pavbaji with chutney

Momos is a chinese dish , Tbh I love them , aaa my mouth is watering right now while writing this

Ingredients

  • Flour - 1kg
  • Water - 500 ml
  • onion
  • cauliflower

Steps

  1. Chop onions and cauliflower and mix them to make stuffing
  2. knead the flour dough
  3. make little circle size pieces using dough
  4. stuff the pudding in the flour dough pieces
  5. take a cooker filled with water
  6. put momos over a plate in cooker
  7. let it steam for 10 minutes
Enjoy!
